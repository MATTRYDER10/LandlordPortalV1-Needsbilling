## Agreed Terms

**1\. Interpretation**

**1.1** The following definitions and rules of interpretation apply in this Agreement.

**Definitions**

**Agreement:** references to 'Agreement' or 'the Agreement' are to this Lodger's Agreement.

**Deposit:** the sum of £\[DEPOSIT\_AMOUNT\] paid by the Lodger to the Landlord as security against the performance of the Lodger's obligations under this Agreement, the discharge of any liabilities, any damage to the Property and/or non-payment of rent during the term of this Agreement.

**Landlord:** the Resident Landlord named above, who resides at the Property and shall include anyone lawfully entitled to the Property upon the termination of this Agreement.

**Lodger:** the person(s) named above who shall occupy the Room as a licensee of the Landlord.

**Permitted Occupiers:** \[PERMITTED\_OCCUPIERS\]. In addition to the Lodger, Permitted Occupiers are the only people permitted by the Landlord to reside at the Property. Permitted Occupiers are not tenants and do not have the rights of tenants under this Agreement.

**Property:** \[PROPERTY ADDRESS\]. References to 'the Property' include the room let to the Lodger and the shared areas described below.

**Room:** \[ROOM\_DESCRIPTION\]. The room within the Property which the Lodger is licensed to occupy exclusively.

**Shared Areas:** \[SHARED\_AREAS\]. These are the areas of the Property which the Lodger is permitted to use in common with the Landlord and any other occupiers of the Property.

**Rent Payment Dates:** For the duration of this Agreement, \[RENT DUE DATE\] of each month. Where the payment date falls on a non-banking day, the Lodger must ensure the payment is received by the Landlord by the next banking day.

**1.2** A person includes a natural person, corporate or unincorporated body (whether or not having separate legal personality).

**1.3** Unless the context otherwise requires, words in the singular shall include the plural and in the plural shall include the singular.

**1.4** A reference to a statute or statutory provision is a reference to it as amended, extended or re-enacted from time to time.

**2\. Grant of Licence**

**2.1** The Landlord grants to the Lodger a licence to occupy the Room at the Property on the terms set out in this Agreement.

**2.2** This Agreement is an excluded licence within the meaning of the Protection from Eviction Act 1977. It does NOT create an assured tenancy, an assured shorthold tenancy, or any other form of tenancy under the Housing Act 1988 or any other legislation. The Lodger acknowledges that they are a licensee and not a tenant.

**2.3** The licence shall commence on \[tenancy\_start\_date\] and shall continue as a \[TERM\_TYPE\] arrangement unless terminated in accordance with the provisions of this Agreement.

**2.4** The Lodger shall not be entitled to exclusive possession of the Property but shall be entitled to exclusive use of the Room, subject to the Landlord's right to enter the Room as set out in this Agreement.

**4\. Deposit**

**4.1** The Lodger shall pay a deposit of £\[DEPOSIT\_AMOUNT\] to the Landlord on or before the commencement of this Agreement.

**4.2** The Deposit is held by the Landlord as security for the performance of the Lodger's obligations under this Agreement and to compensate the Landlord for any breach of those obligations. The Deposit is NOT required to be protected in a tenancy deposit protection scheme as this Agreement is an excluded licence and not an assured tenancy.

**4.3** The Landlord shall return the Deposit (or the balance thereof after any lawful deductions) to the Lodger within \[DEPOSIT\_RETURN\_DAYS\] days of the termination of this Agreement and the Lodger vacating the Property, provided that the Landlord shall be entitled to make reasonable deductions from the Deposit in respect of:

(a) any damage to the Property or the Fixtures and Fittings (beyond fair wear and tear) caused by the Lodger or any person the Lodger has allowed into the Property;

(b) any rent or other sums due and unpaid under this Agreement;

(c) any reasonable costs incurred by the Landlord in cleaning the Room and shared areas to the standard they were in at the commencement of this Agreement (beyond fair wear and tear);

(d) any other breach of the Lodger's obligations under this Agreement.

**4.4** The Landlord shall provide the Lodger with a written statement of any deductions made from the Deposit together with any supporting evidence within a reasonable time.

**6\. Lodger's Obligations**

**6.1** The Lodger agrees:

(a) to pay the rent on the due date and in the manner specified in this Agreement;

(b) to keep the Room clean, tidy and in good condition and to leave it in the same condition (fair wear and tear excepted) at the end of this Agreement;

(c) to use the shared areas in a reasonable manner and to keep them clean and tidy after use;

(d) not to cause or permit any nuisance, annoyance, disturbance, or inconvenience to the Landlord or to any neighbours or occupiers of adjoining properties;

(e) not to use the Property or allow it to be used for any illegal or immoral purpose;

(f) not to keep any pets at the Property without the prior written consent of the Landlord;

(g) not to smoke inside the Property unless the Landlord has given prior written consent;

(h) not to make any alterations or additions to the Room or the Property without the prior written consent of the Landlord;

(i) not to assign, sublet, or part with possession of the Room or any part of the Property;

(j) not to display any notice, sign, or advertisement at the Property;

(k) to allow the Landlord reasonable access to the Room for the purposes of inspection, repair, or maintenance, at reasonable times and with reasonable notice (except in the case of emergency);

(l) to report promptly to the Landlord any damage to the Property or any disrepair or defect in the Property;

(m) to comply with all reasonable house rules set by the Landlord from time to time;

(n) not to change the locks on any doors at the Property without the prior written consent of the Landlord;

(o) to provide the Landlord with a forwarding address at the end of this Agreement for the purposes of returning the Deposit and any correspondence;

(p) not to allow any person other than the Permitted Occupiers to reside at the Property without the prior written consent of the Landlord;

(q) to observe and comply with all laws, regulations, and requirements affecting the Property.

---

**7\. Landlord's Obligations**

**7.1** The Landlord agrees:

(a) to allow the Lodger to occupy the Room and use the shared areas peacefully without unnecessary interruption or interference, provided the Lodger complies with the terms of this Agreement;

(b) to keep the structure and exterior of the Property in good repair;

(c) to keep in repair and proper working order the installations in the Property for the supply of water, gas, electricity, sanitation, and for space and water heating;

(d) to ensure that the Property is fit for human habitation at the commencement of the Agreement and throughout its duration;

(e) to provide adequate facilities for the Lodger's use of the shared areas;

(f) to give reasonable notice before entering the Room (except in an emergency);

(g) to insure the Property and its contents (excluding the Lodger's personal belongings) against standard insured risks;

(h) to return the Deposit in accordance with the provisions of this Agreement.

---

**8\. Landlord's Right of Entry**

**8.1** The Landlord retains the right to enter the Room at reasonable times and with reasonable notice for the purposes of:

(a) inspecting the condition of the Room;

(b) carrying out repairs or maintenance to the Room or the Property;

(c) in the case of emergency.

**8.2** Except in the case of emergency, the Landlord shall give the Lodger at least 24 hours' written notice before entering the Room.

---

**9\. Termination**

**9.1** The Landlord may terminate this Agreement by giving the Lodger not less than \[LANDLORD\_NOTICE\_DAYS\] days' written notice. As this is an excluded licence, the Landlord is not required to obtain a court order for possession but must give reasonable notice.

**9.2** The Lodger may terminate this Agreement by giving the Landlord not less than \[LODGER\_NOTICE\_DAYS\] days' written notice.

**9.3** The Landlord may terminate this Agreement immediately or on short notice if:

(a) the Lodger fails to pay the rent within 14 days of the due date;

(b) the Lodger is in serious breach of any of the Lodger's obligations under this Agreement;

(c) the Lodger's behaviour is such that it is unreasonable for the Landlord to continue to allow the Lodger to reside at the Property.

**9.4** Upon termination of this Agreement, the Lodger shall vacate the Room and the Property and remove all personal belongings. Any belongings left at the Property after 14 days following termination may be disposed of by the Landlord.

**9.5** Termination of this Agreement shall not affect any rights or obligations which have accrued prior to termination.
