## 1. NATURE OF AGREEMENT

1.1 This agreement is a holiday let within the meaning of paragraph 9 of Schedule 1 to the Housing Act 1988. The purpose of this letting is to confer on the Guest the right to occupy the Property for a holiday.

1.2 This agreement does not create an assured tenancy or an assured shorthold tenancy. The Guest shall have no security of tenure and no right to remain in the Property after the check-out date.

1.3 The Renters' Rights Act 2025 (formerly Renters' Reform Bill) does not apply to this agreement.

## 2. TERM AND PAYMENT

2.1 The Owner agrees to let and the Guest agrees to take the Property for the period from [CHECK_IN_DATE] to [CHECK_OUT_DATE] ("the Booking Period").

2.2 The total rental charge for the Booking Period is £[RENT_AMOUNT].

2.3 A non-refundable booking deposit of £[DEPOSIT_AMOUNT] is due at the time of booking to secure the reservation. If the balance is not received by the due date, the Owner reserves the right to treat the booking as cancelled and retain the booking deposit.

## 3. SECURITY DEPOSIT

3.1 A refundable security deposit of £[DEPOSIT_AMOUNT] is payable prior to or on arrival.

3.2 The security deposit will be returned to the Guest within 14 days of check-out, less any deductions for:

- Damage to the Property, its fixtures, fittings, furniture or contents beyond fair wear and tear
- Additional cleaning costs where the Property is not left in a reasonably clean and tidy condition
- Replacement of lost keys, fobs or security devices
- Any unpaid charges or costs arising from breach of this agreement

3.3 The Owner shall provide an itemised schedule of any deductions. The security deposit is not held under a tenancy deposit scheme as this is a holiday let.

## 4. ARRIVAL AND DEPARTURE

4.1 The Guest may check in from [CHECK_IN_TIME] on [CHECK_IN_DATE].

4.2 The Guest must check out by [CHECK_OUT_TIME] on [CHECK_OUT_DATE].

4.3 Late check-out without the Owner's prior written consent may result in an additional charge.

4.4 The Owner (or the Owner's representative) will provide access arrangements, keys and any entry codes prior to arrival.

## 5. GUEST'S OBLIGATIONS

The Guest agrees to:

5.1 Use the Property solely as holiday or temporary accommodation and for no other purpose.

5.2 Not exceed the maximum occupancy of [MAX_OCCUPANCY] persons at any time (excluding day visitors).

5.3 Keep the Property and all furniture, fixtures, fittings and effects in the same state of repair and condition as at the commencement of the Booking Period (fair wear and tear excepted).

5.4 Leave the Property in a reasonably clean and tidy condition at the end of the Booking Period, with all crockery, cutlery and kitchen equipment washed and returned to their proper places, bins emptied and all rubbish disposed of correctly.

5.5 Report any breakages, damage or malfunction of any equipment to the Owner as soon as reasonably practicable.

5.6 Not smoke inside the Property.

5.7 Not bring any pets to the Property without the Owner's prior written consent.

5.8 Not cause any nuisance, disturbance or annoyance to neighbours or nearby residents. Excessive noise is not permitted after 10:00 PM.

5.9 Not hold any parties, events or gatherings at the Property without the Owner's prior written consent.

5.10 Not sublet, share or assign the Property or any part of it.

5.11 Not use the Property for any illegal or immoral purpose.

5.12 Comply with any house rules provided by the Owner.

5.13 Ensure all windows and doors are locked and secure when the Property is unoccupied and upon departure.

5.14 Return all keys, fobs and access devices on departure. A charge of £50 will apply for lost keys or replacement locks.

## 6. OWNER'S OBLIGATIONS

The Owner agrees to:

6.1 Ensure the Property is clean, safe and in good order at the start of the Booking Period.

6.2 Provide bed linen, towels and basic cleaning materials unless otherwise agreed.

6.3 Ensure all gas and electrical appliances at the Property are safe and in proper working order.

6.4 Hold a valid Gas Safety Certificate, Energy Performance Certificate and Electrical Installation Condition Report (where applicable).

6.5 Ensure working smoke alarms and carbon monoxide detectors are installed at the Property.

6.6 Maintain adequate buildings insurance for the Property. The Guest is responsible for insuring their own personal belongings.

6.7 Respond to maintenance or repair requests within a reasonable timeframe during the Booking Period.

## 7. RIGHT OF ACCESS

7.1 The Owner (or the Owner's representative) reserves the right to enter the Property at reasonable times for the purpose of essential repairs or in an emergency.

7.2 Except in the case of emergency, the Owner shall give reasonable notice before accessing the Property during the Booking Period.

## 8. LIABILITY

8.1 The Owner shall not be liable for any loss, damage or injury to the Guest or the Guest's personal property arising from the Guest's use of the Property, except where caused by the Owner's negligence.

8.2 The Owner shall not be liable for any temporary failure or interruption of gas, electricity, water, broadband or other services or facilities at the Property due to circumstances beyond the Owner's control.

8.3 The Guest shall be responsible for, and shall indemnify the Owner against, any loss or damage to the Property and its contents caused by the Guest or any member of the Guest's party.

## 9. GENERAL PROVISIONS

9.1 This agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties in relation to the holiday let of the Property.

9.2 No variation of this agreement shall be effective unless agreed in writing by both parties.

9.3 Any notice required under this agreement shall be in writing and may be given by email to the addresses stated above.

9.4 If any provision of this agreement is found to be invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.

9.5 This agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of England and Wales.

## 10. DATA PROTECTION

10.1 The Owner will hold personal data relating to the Guest in connection with the management of this booking. Such data will be processed in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

10.2 The Guest's data will only be used for the purposes of managing the booking and will not be shared with third parties except where required by law or for the management of the Property.
