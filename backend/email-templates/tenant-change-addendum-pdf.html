4 Terms of Assignment

It is hereby agreed that:

  1. With effect from the Changeover Date, the Outgoing Tenant(s) hereby assign all their rights, title, interest and obligations under the Tenancy Agreement to the Incoming Tenant(s).
  2. The Incoming Tenant(s) agree to be bound by and perform all the covenants, conditions and obligations contained in the Tenancy Agreement as if they were original parties thereto.
  3. The Outgoing Tenant(s) are released from all further obligations under the Tenancy Agreement from the Changeover Date, provided all rent and other sums due up to that date have been paid in full.
  4. The Incoming Tenant(s) shall be jointly and severally liable with any Remaining Tenant(s) for all obligations under the Tenancy Agreement from the Changeover Date.
  5. The Incoming Tenant(s) accept the current condition of the Property and acknowledge the existing inventory report and schedule of condition as an accurate record of the Property's state at the date of this Addendum.
  6. All other terms and conditions of the original Tenancy Agreement remain in full force and effect.
  7. The Landlord/Agent consents to this assignment and confirms that it does not constitute a breach of the Tenancy Agreement.
Important: This Addendum forms a legally binding supplement to the existing Tenancy Agreement. All parties should retain a copy for their records. The Prescribed Information will be re-served to the new tenant composition within 30 days.
