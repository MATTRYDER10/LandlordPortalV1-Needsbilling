What is a Guarantor?

A guarantor is someone who agrees to pay your rent and cover any damages if you are unable to do so. This is usually a parent, close relative, or trusted friend who:

  • Is a UK resident or homeowner
  • Has a stable income and good credit history
  • Is willing to undergo identity and credit checks
  • Understands the legal responsibility of being a guarantor
