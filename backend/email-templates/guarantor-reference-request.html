What is a Guarantor?

A guarantor is someone who agrees to cover the rent and other tenancy costs if the tenant cannot pay. This is a legally binding agreement, and you should only proceed if:

  • You are financially able to pay the rent if required
  • You understand the full extent of your legal obligations
  • You trust the tenant to fulfill their responsibilities
  • You are willing to undergo identity and credit checks

What You Need to Provide:

  • Proof of Identity - Passport, driving license, or national ID
  • Proof of Address - Utility bill, bank statement, or council tax letter
  • Employment & Income Details - Employer information and salary details
  • Bank Statements - Last 3 months to verify financial capability
  • Property Ownership Details - If you own property
  • Consent to credit and background checks
