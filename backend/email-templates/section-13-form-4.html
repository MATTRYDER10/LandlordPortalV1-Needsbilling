Important Notes for Tenant(s)

This notice proposes a new rent. If you want to object or have this notice referred to a tribunal, you must do so before the date the new rent is due to start.

  • You may accept the proposed rent by doing nothing. If you do not apply to the tribunal before the start date, the rent proposed in this notice will become payable.
  • If you think the proposed rent is too high compared to similar properties in your area, you can apply to the First-tier Tribunal (Property Chamber) for them to decide a market rent for your property.
  • You can get an application form online at www.gov.uk/housing-tribunal or by contacting the tribunal.
  • The tribunal may set a rent that is higher, lower, or the same as the proposed rent.
  • If you apply to the tribunal, the rent set by the tribunal will apply from the date given in paragraph 3 above.
